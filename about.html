About

Divo's music journey

Divo started its music journey in 2014, as a startup music record label and distributor working out a model to disrupt and innovate with the ever growing digital landscape in music. Our vision, mission and promise from day one has always been to be transparent across all aspects of the business, be it the agreements, the work we do for you, and the reporting for payments to ensure our clients and partners

build a trust and relationship with us for the long term. We have grown to learn about this industry and continue to learn and innovate to help YOU get the best. We also believe that anyone who knocks our door will get the opportunity to release their music with no discrimination or rejection We respect everyone's talent and we and our team of professionals want to help serve you to get your music out.

Get your music out worldwide and earn royalties across all possible revenue and royalty stream
